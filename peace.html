🌟 About the Initiative

I founded and led the Wazir-Abad community initiative as part of the International Cities of Peace network. My goal is to connect my local community with global peace-building efforts and promote education, cooperation, and positive development.

👤 My Role

As the community leader, I gathered information about Wazir-Abad, documented the community's identity, collected photos, and created a profile to represent my community on an international platform.

  • Founder and community leader
  • Collected and organized community information
  • Documented local strengths and needs
  • Connected my community with an international peace network

🌍 Vision for Wazir-Abad

I believe peaceful communities are built through education, respect, technology, and cooperation. Through this initiative, I hope to encourage youth involvement and create awareness about community development.

🎯 Future Goals

  • Support education and digital skills development
  • Encourage youth leadership
  • Create more community improvement projects
  • Use technology to create positive social impact
